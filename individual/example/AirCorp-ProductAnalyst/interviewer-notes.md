# Interviewer Notes
**Role:** Product Analyst — Platform Modernization
**Hiring Manager:** Jordan Blake
**Interviewer:** Jordan Blake
**Date:** 2026-01

---

## What I Need to Learn

This role will work directly with our engineering leads and business stakeholders. The candidate needs to bridge both worlds — they can't be purely technical or purely business-facing.

## Focus Areas for This Interview

1. **Migration experience** — Have they actually worked on a platform or data migration? Not just adjacent to one — hands-on. Probe for specifics: what was migrated, what went wrong, how they handled it.

2. **Stakeholder communication under pressure** — Our migration is politically complex. I need to know how they handle disagreement between technical and business stakeholders. Ask for a real example.

3. **Ambiguity tolerance** — Requirements will change. Priorities will shift. Do they get frustrated and slow down, or do they adapt and keep moving?

4. **Documentation discipline** — We have almost no current-state documentation. The first 90 days will be heavily documentation-focused. Make sure they're genuinely good at this, not just claiming to be.

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Candidates who talk process but can't give specifics
- Anyone who seems to need a lot of direction to get started
- Vague answers about migration experience — if they were "involved" but can't describe their actual work, that's a concern
