# Job Description — Product Analyst, Platform Modernization
**Company:** AirCorp
**Hiring Manager:** Jordan Blake, Engineering Director
**Posted:** 2026-01

---

## About the Role

AirCorp is modernizing its core platform — migrating legacy systems to a modern data architecture while maintaining continuity for enterprise customers. We're looking for a Product Analyst to lead requirements gathering, stakeholder communication, and data analysis throughout the migration.

## What You'll Do

- Own requirements gathering across business and technical stakeholders for platform migration workstreams
- Document current-state processes and translate them into future-state specifications
- Analyze data to identify migration risks, gaps, and opportunities
- Communicate progress and decisions to executive and operational audiences
- Drive continuous process improvement across the migration program

## What We're Looking For

- 4+ years in a product, business analysis, or program management role
- Experience with large-scale platform or data migration projects
- Strong documentation and communication skills — written and verbal
- Ability to work across technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Proficiency with data analysis tools (SQL, Excel, or equivalent)
- Familiarity with agile/iterative delivery

## Key Competencies

1. Requirements Gathering
2. Current-State Documentation
3. Data Analysis & Synthesis
4. Stakeholder Communication
5. Platform Migration Experience
6. Process Improvement
7. Technical Tool Proficiency
