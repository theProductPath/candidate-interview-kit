1. "Walk me through your most complex Snowflake query at Tempo. What were you trying to answer, how did you structure the query, and what did you find?"
2. "If I gave you a new dataset you'd never seen before — say, AirCorp's migration tracking data — what's your first hour look like?"
3. "Describe the data model at Tempo Labs. How were the tables structured? What joins did you use most often?"
4. "Tell me about a time your data analysis contradicted what the team believed. What happened?"
5. "What's the difference between reporting and analysis? Give me an example of each from your work."
6. "How do you validate that your analysis is correct before sharing it with stakeholders?"
