## Recommended Probe Areas

1. **Architecture beyond Salesforce** — His entire career is one platform. Can he reason about system architecture outside that ecosystem?
2. **Integration thinking** — The Sales Cloud consolidation involved merging instances. Did he think about the integration architecture or just the business rules?
3. **Transformation vs. optimization** — Every example is making Salesforce better. Has he ever questioned whether Salesforce is the right answer?

## Suggested Questions

1. "Walk me through the architecture of the Sales Cloud consolidation. How did the six regional instances connect to other systems?"
2. "If you were asked to evaluate whether Salesforce was the right platform for a new use case, how would you approach that?"
3. "Describe an API or integration you've worked with. What did it connect and why did it exist?"
4. "What's the difference between optimizing a workflow within Salesforce and rethinking whether that workflow should exist at all?"
5. "If I gave you access to a system you've never seen before, what's your approach to understanding its architecture?"

## Watch For

- Whether he can discuss architecture concepts beyond Salesforce data model terminology
- How he responds to questions about unfamiliar platforms — curiosity vs. deflection
- Whether he ever questions platform decisions or only optimizes within them
