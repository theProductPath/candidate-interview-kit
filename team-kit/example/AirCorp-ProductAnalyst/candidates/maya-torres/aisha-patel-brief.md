1. "Walk me through the ServiceNow Hardware Selection improvement. You mentioned measuring results — what exactly did you measure, and how?"

4. "What's your comfort level with SQL? Write me a query in plain English that would answer a question about migration readiness."
5. "When you're building a case for a process change, what's the balance between data and stakeholder interviews in your approach?"
6. "Tell me about the most complex dataset you've worked with. What made it complex and what did you learn from it?"
